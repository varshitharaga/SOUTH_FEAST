Mango Milkshake

INGREDIENTS:

  • 2 large mango
  • 4 scoops vanilla ice cream
  • 4 piece cashews
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 tablespoon sugar
  • 4 pieces almonds

Method :

  • Firstly, peel the mangoes and chop the pulp into small pieces. Scrape out the remaining pulp and collect it in a bowl.
  • Add the mango pulp to a blender, along with chilled milk, sugar and 2 scoops of vanilla ice cream. Blend to form a smooth mixture.
  • Now pour the milkshake into two different glasses and fill them 3/4th. Add a scoop of vanilla ice cream to each glass.
  • Garnish with roughly chopped cashews, almonds and serve.
